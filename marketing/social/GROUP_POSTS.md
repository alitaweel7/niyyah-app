# Niyyah (نِيّة) — Facebook / community group posts

Copy for sharing Niyyah into Facebook (and similar) groups. Written in a warm, humble
maker voice, value-first, no em dashes. Two kinds:

1. **Group-safe posts (no link, no store mention)** — safest for groups that ban promotion.
2. **Bilingual share post (with App Store link)** — for groups where a link is allowed.

Replace `APP_STORE_LINK` with the real listing URL when you use the link version. If a
group blocks links in the body, put the link in the first comment instead
("link in the comments" / "الرابط في التعليقات").

---

## 1. Group-safe posts — NO link, NO store mention

Two standalone posts (post the one matching the group's language, or both in a mixed group).
Describes what Niyyah is: daily dua, weekly/monthly Qur'an completion plan, the pause-before-you-scroll
gate, prayer tracking, Qur'an progress, qibla.

### English

```
Assalamu alaykum 🌙

I want to share something I've been working on that has helped me bring more Qur'an into my day. It's an app called Niyyah.

The idea is simple: keep the Qur'an and your prayers close, even in the middle of a busy day.

A few things it helps with:
• A daily dua to read, so you always have a supplication from the Sunnah on your tongue.
• A Qur'an reading plan that breaks the whole Qur'an into small daily portions, so you can complete it on a weekly or monthly schedule without it ever feeling heavy.
• Before you open a distracting app, it shows you an ayah to read first and lets you set your intention, so some of your scrolling time quietly turns into Qur'an time.
• Gentle tracking for your five daily prayers, your progress through the Qur'an, and a compass for the qibla.

It genuinely changed how my phone feels. Less mindless scrolling, more peace.

May Allah make us all of the people of the Qur'an. 🤍
```

## Posting tips
- Check each group's rules first. Many ban promotion outright but allow it on a set day or with admin permission. The humble maker voice usually passes where an ad would not.
- To stay honest as the maker without sounding like an ad, keep the "I've been working on / I built" framing. Swap to "we" for a brand voice.
- If asked where to get it, drop the name (or link) in a reply rather than editing store/link details into the original post.
