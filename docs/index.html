How It Works

A gentle gate between you and distraction

Niyyah creates a calm reading experience every time you reach for social media. Three simple steps to more intentional screen time.

1

Choose Your Apps

Select which apps you want to pause before opening. Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, and more.

2

Read Before You Scroll

When you open a gated app, Niyyah presents a calm reading experience with Quran, Duas, or Hadith for 1 to 10 minutes.

3

Continue Mindfully

After your reading time, the app unlocks temporarily. You have set your intention and earned your scroll.

Support

Frequently asked questions

How does Niyyah work? +
When you open a gated app like Instagram or TikTok, Niyyah intercepts the launch and presents a calm reading screen. You spend your chosen amount of time reading Quran, Duas, or Hadith. After your reading time is complete, the app unlocks for you to use.
Which apps can I gate? +
You can choose any app installed on your device. Popular choices include Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X (Twitter), Snapchat, and Reddit, but you have full control over which apps require a reading pause.
Does Niyyah collect any of my data? +
No. All data stays entirely on your device. Niyyah has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. We cannot see what you read, when you read, or which apps you use. Your privacy is a core promise, not an afterthought.
Is the Quran text authentic? +
Yes. The Quran text is sourced from the Tanzil Project (tanzil.net) in Uthmani script, with translations available in 9 languages. The English translation uses the widely respected Sahih International edition.
Is the app free? +
Niyyah is free to download with no in-app purchases. There are no ads and no premium tiers. The app is built as a spiritual product, not a commercial one.
