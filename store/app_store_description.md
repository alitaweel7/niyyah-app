# App Store / Play Store Listing

## App Name
Niyyah

## Subtitle (App Store) / Short Description (Play Store)
Set your niyyah before you scroll

## Description

Niyyah helps you pause before you scroll.

Instead of mindlessly opening Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube, Niyyah places a calm Quran or Dua reading experience between you and your social media apps. Spend a few minutes with the words of Allah before continuing to your app.

This is not another app blocker. There are no guilt trips, no gamification, no ads. Niyyah is a spiritual intentionality tool designed for Muslims who want to reclaim their attention with purpose.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Choose which apps to gate (Instagram, TikTok, X, Snapchat, Facebook, YouTube)
2. When you open a gated app, a Quran or Dua reading screen appears
3. Spend time with the reading (default 5 minutes)
4. After the timer, choose to keep reading or continue to your app
5. Your app unlocks temporarily, then the gate returns

WHAT YOU'LL READ

- Full Quran text in beautiful Uthmani Arabic script with English translation
- 76+ authentic Duas from the Quran and Sunnah with source references
- Three Quran modes: Short Surahs, Random Ayahs, or Sequential Reading
- Track your progress toward completing the entire Quran through daily gates

DESIGNED WITH RESPECT

- Beautiful Arabic typography using the Amiri font
- Calm, minimal design with generous whitespace
- Dark mode for comfortable nighttime reading
- No ads. No data collection. No manipulation.
- All reading data stays on your device

HONEST ABOUT LIMITATIONS

We cannot verify that you truly read the Quran. We can only offer a meaningful pause. Niyyah is designed for people who want the pause — not for enforcement.

FREE FEATURES

- Gate up to 3 apps
- Full Quran gate experience
- Full Dua gate experience
- Configurable timer (1-10 minutes)
- Reading history and streak tracking
- Dark mode
